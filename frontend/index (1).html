Emotion + tumour link: The affective EEG encoder was trained on DEAP valence/arousal/dominance labels and maps 32-channel EEG to four negative emotion states — neutral, sadness, fear, distress. This signal contributes to both the emotion head and, via cross-modal attention in the fusion model, to the tumour classification — reflecting clinical evidence that patient emotional state correlates with tumour grade.
